SECTION 7 — WHAT YOU ACTUALLY WANT
THE HONEST ANSWER — CONSENSUS ACROSS ALL AGENTS
The pull toward imagery is real. It is partly aesthetic (the mockups looked compelling), partly behavioral (a richer app is easier to open), and partly the system-building pattern INCREMENTS' own guardrails name explicitly — designing the cathedral instead of using the clinic.

What this profile actually needs is not visual richness. It is the feeling that the system is alive — quietly, without demanding attention. That is achievable through data density, typographic scale, material depth, and restrained symbolic continuity. It is not achievable through conventional imagery without introducing risks that outweigh the benefit.

The science-based answer: do not add imagery now. Build the system. Use it for 30 days. Let the data become the atmosphere. From a position of established practice, evaluate from evidence — not from a high-motivation design session.
"participation in reality" — not participation in a beautiful simulation of it
